# Predictions ledger

This is your moat. Every issue that makes a call records it here with a date and a confidence
level. Every quarter you grade the open calls in public. Almost no one in this niche keeps
score on themselves, so doing it sets you apart and builds the kind of trust that senior
buyers act on.

## How to use it

1. When an issue makes a prediction, copy it here the day you publish. Fill in the date, the
   claim, the time horizon, the confidence, and the link to the issue.
2. Leave the outcome blank.
3. Each quarter, revisit every prediction whose horizon has passed. Mark it Correct, Wrong,
   or Partly right, and write one honest line on what you learned. Then publish the review.
4. Never edit a past prediction. The value is that it is a fixed record. If you change your
   mind, log a new one and explain the update.

## Grading scale

- **Correct**: the claim held as stated.
- **Partly right**: the direction held, the specifics or timing did not.
- **Wrong**: it did not happen. Say so plainly. A wrong call honestly graded builds more
  trust than a vague one.
- **Open**: the horizon has not passed yet.
